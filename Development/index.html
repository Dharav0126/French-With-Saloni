Bonjour, je suis Saloni

With an M.A. in French Literature from the Sorbonne and over eight years teaching across three continents, Saloni has guided thousands of students from their very first "bonjour" to near-native fluency. Her method blends rigorous linguistics with storytelling, music, and cultural richness.

  • M.A. French Literature, Université Paris-Sorbonne
  • Certified DELF / DALF Examiner
  • 8+ years teaching A1 through C1 levels
  • Host of "Café Français" podcast — 200K+ listeners
  • Featured in Le Monde, BBC Languages & Duolingo Blog
